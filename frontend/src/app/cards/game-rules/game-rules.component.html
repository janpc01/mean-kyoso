Rules of Play

  • "Shiai" Setup: Shuffle the "Shiai" stack of Action Cards and place them between players.
  • "Bow In": Each player places a Collector Player Card face up (Judo Technique Side).
  • "Hajime": Player 2 cuts the "Shiai" stack.
  • "First Move": The player with the higher-point card draws the first Action Card.
  • "The Match": Players alternate drawing cards from the stack.
  • "Conversion": Instead of drawing a card, a player can convert cards to points or penalties.
  • "Win": Follow the "3 Ways to Win" outlined above.

Game Modes

Play for Keeps

Reveal the wagered cards and keep the one you win.

Play for Fun

Reveal the wagered cards, but everyone keeps their collection.
